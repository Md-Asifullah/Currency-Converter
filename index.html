Currency Converter


This is a very simple currecny converter app made with html, css and javascrpit. This converter works with three currencies: United States Dollar, Malaysian Ringgit and Bangladeshi Taka. The conversion rates are hardcorded in line 4 and 5 of the JavaScript file. For real time value conversion please update those values using the button on the top right corner prior to performing your operations.


This is very simple to use. Click on any 3 of the buttons. Then enter the amount you want to convert. Finally press the Convert button.
